Core Infrastructure
HIS / HIMS
Hospital Information Management System

The central brain that runs everything — from patient records, appointments, and doctor notes to billing and bed management. It unifies the full patient journey across all departments, eliminating administrative silos.

Allows doctors to focus entirely on patients, not paperwork.
Imaging Infrastructure
PACS
Picture Archiving & Communication System

A secure, cloud-like system that stores all X-rays and scans so doctors can view them instantly from anywhere. Fully filmless — eliminating costly consumables and enabling faster diagnosis.

Saves up to 70% on consumables vs. traditional film-based systems.
Remote Care
Teleradiology
Built on PACS / RICS

Allows expert radiologists in one location to read and diagnose scans for patients in another hospital or clinic — expanding access to specialist care across any geography without compromise.

Improves access to care and reduces time-to-diagnosis across regions.
AI Diagnostics
X-Eye AI Suite
AI Diagnostic Tools

An AI assistant that helps doctors analyze medical images to spot diseases like breast cancer, lung nodules, and pneumonia earlier and more accurately. A second pair of expert eyes on every scan.

High-accuracy early detection that supports — not replaces — the radiologist.
Critical Care
iVital
Intensive Care Unit System

Automatically collects all patient monitor data, spots early warning signs, and sends alerts to the medical team's mobile devices to prevent crises. Centralized ICU monitoring that automates manual charting.

A critical safety net for early intervention that directly improves outcomes.
Chronic Care
Teledialysis (Nefro)
Dialysis Management System

A system and patient mobile app that manages dialysis treatment and keeps patients connected to their care team from home — transforming patients into active partners in their own healthcare journey.

Improves engagement, adherence, and long-term patient health outcomes.
Analytics
HIS Dashboards
Real-Time Hospital Intelligence

Real-time screens that show how the entire hospital is performing — from bed occupancy and ER wait times to ICU ventilator usage and average length of stay — enabling faster, smarter decisions at every level.

The proof your digital transformation is working, visible in real time.
